Introduction

The Providential Mathematics Olympiad (PMO) is a challenging olympiad-style mathematics competition which focuses on the development of problem-solving, creative and logical thinking skills. What makes this competition unique is that it requires competitors to communicate their ideas through a variety of formats, and formally encourages the exchange of thoughts during the competition process. We believe these are important skills that aspiring mathematicians should acquire at an early stage. In addition, such experiences are often a valuable learning process that cannot be achieved by solving a lot of problems.
